Axiom Fleet Intelligence — AI-powered profit prediction and fleet management for freight operators.

Make smarter job decisions in seconds. Before accepting any load, Axiom predicts your exact profit margin factoring in fuel, driver costs, tolls, maintenance, and more — powered by machine learning trained on your own fleet's history.

<b>KEY FEATURES</b>

🚛 <b>Instant Profit Prediction</b>
Submit a job and get a full cost breakdown with net profit, margin %, and risk level before you commit.

✅ <b>Smart Accept / Reject</b>
AI recommendation tells you whether to take the job based on your historical margins and current costs.

📊 <b>Fleet Dashboard</b>
Live overview of revenue, profit, accept rate, and margin trends across your entire operation.

📈 <b>Analytics Deep-Dive</b>
Monthly profit trends, top-performing routes, and fuel volatility impact charts.

🔮 <b>What-If Scenarios</b>
Model the financial impact of fuel price changes, rate shifts, or adding trucks before making decisions.

👥 <b>Team Management</b>
Invite dispatchers and viewers with role-based access control.

📶 <b>Works Offline</b>
Core app functions are available even without an internet connection.

<b>DESIGNED FOR</b>
Independent owner-operators, small fleet managers, and dispatchers who want data-driven confidence on every job decision.
